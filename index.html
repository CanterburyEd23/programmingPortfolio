About Me

Hi there, and welcome to my portfolio website. I'm Ed Hampton, a Canterbury based programmer who is very much a new entrant into the exciting world of programming, having recently left a career as a chef to study full-time. I'm currently part way through Codecademy's Full-Stack Engineer course, though this is just one section of the package of re-training courses I am undertaking in order to switch careers. Much as the leap to leave a safe and familiar job for the unknown was a big one, I am pleased with my progress so far, and excited about the new skills I'm learning and the possibilities for my future that they bring!

About this Website

This Website is my portfolio, such as it is. Currently, this is largely projects completed as part of my Codecademy course, many of them built with a lot of hand-holding and to very precise specifications, leaving little room to show any creativity or individual skill beyond ability to follow instructions. However, as I progress, more interesting course projects, and some personal projects, are being added!
